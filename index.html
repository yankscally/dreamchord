I did not expect to be here now. Things have changed forever. Moving on is good. Moving on is hard. Doing things differently. Everyone rushing towards the same thing. Traffic jams and congestion. Follow the signs. Follow the lights, stare at the screens forever. Look at the words and become them. Open your arms to me. There are more flowers growing today than ever before. You are a human, you have dreams and you should follow them. Everyone deserves to dream. Wait a while and count the days not the hours. The patience will be painful but important. Good life taken for granted too much. Existence is important. The universe has feelings, don't hurt them. We have the privilege to look after the planet and creatures it gave us. I feel so young and ready to help. The world we made was moving too fast. The unbreakable engine of commerce has broken down, and a new age of enlightenment will take its place. Put your dreams there. The rat race has finished. The 'show must go on' they will say. Ignore them. Look towards a bright future filled with happiness and nature. The dreams must go on. The idea of utopia is not a place we build in reality. It comes from within ourselves. We have to make the utopia in our minds. There is no return to normal after so why not make it good.

Yank Scally


Dreamchord Operator
